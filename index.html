About Kelsey Days

Kelsey Days is an annual celebration that brings together our community for unforgettable moments of joy, culture, and shared experiences.

Since its inception, Kelsey Days has been a cornerstone event, fostering connections and creating lasting memories. Our celebration features a diverse array of activities, performances, and experiences designed to engage and inspire attendees of all ages.

Join us as we continue this cherished tradition, honoring our heritage while embracing the future with open arms and warm hearts.
